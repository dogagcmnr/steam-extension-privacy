Privacy Policy - Steam Purchase History Pro

Last Updated: May 2026

Overview

Steam Purchase History Pro ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

A. Google Account Information (Collected at Login)

  • Email address - For account identification
  • Full name - For display and personalization
  • Profile picture - For UI display (optional)

B. Usage & Activity Data (Collected During Use)

  • Export timestamps - When you export data
  • Sheet names - What you name your exports
  • Item counts - How many Steam items you exported
  • Links to your sheets - URLs of your Google Sheets
  • Login timestamps - When you use the extension

C. Steam Data (You Control This)

When you export, the Extension reads your Steam purchase history from store.steampowered.com. This data is:

  • ✅ Only sent to Google Sheets (your sheet, in your Drive)
  • ✅ Not sent to our servers
  • ✅ Not logged or tracked
  • ✅ Fully under your control

2. What We DON'T Collect

  • ✗ Your Steam ID or Steam profile data
  • ✗ Your passwords or authentication tokens
  • ✗ Your IP address or location
  • ✗ Device information or hardware details
  • ✗ Browser history or browsing behavior
  • ✗ Any data beyond what's listed above

3. How We Use Your Information

A. Primary Purpose

Your information is used to:

  • Enable the core functionality (exporting Steam data to Google Sheets)
  • Identify your account when you return
  • Show your name in the extension interface

B. Analytics & Improvement

We collect usage statistics (email, name, export timestamps, sheet links) to:

  • Understand how the extension is being used
  • Identify which features are most valuable
  • Track export frequency and patterns
  • Improve the extension based on real usage

C. We Do NOT Use Your Data For:

  • ✗ Advertising or marketing
  • ✗ Selling or sharing with third parties
  • ✗ Profiling or behavioral tracking
  • ✗ Any purpose other than above

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Usage Analytics

Your export activity (email, name, timestamps, sheet links) is logged in a Google Sheet owned by the Extension developer. This sheet is:

  • ✓ Encrypted at rest (Google's security)
  • ✓ HTTPS encrypted in transit
  • ✓ Only accessible to the developer
  • ✓ Never shared with third parties

Your Steam Data

Steam data you export goes directly to:

  • Your Google Sheets (in your Google Drive)
  • ✓ Fully under your control
  • ✓ Only you can access
  • ✓ Never accessed by us

Authentication

Google OAuth tokens are:

  • Managed by Chrome (encrypted storage)
  • Automatically expire and refresh
  • Never stored by the Extension permanently

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We NEVER:

  • ✗ Sell your data
  • ✗ Share with third parties
  • ✗ Use data brokers
  • ✗ Give access to your information
  • ✗ Monetize your data

We DO use:

  • ✓ Google Sheets API (to create your sheets)
  • ✓ Google Drive API (to store your sheets)
  • ✓ Google OAuth (for authentication)

All are first-party integrations with Google services.

6. Your Privacy Rights

A. Access Your Data

You can request to see all data we have about you. Contact the developer.

B. Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your account and all associated analytics data. Email the developer with your request.

C. Revoke Extension Access

You can revoke access anytime:

  • Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Find "Steam Purchase History Pro"
  • Click "Remove access"

D. Uninstall Extension

Simply uninstall the extension from Chrome. All locally cached data is immediately removed.

E. Right to Explanation

You have the right to understand how your data is used. This privacy policy explains everything.

7. Data Retention

How long we keep data:

  • Usage analytics: Kept indefinitely (unless you request deletion)
  • Google Sheets: Stored in your Drive (you control retention)
  • Auth tokens: Only while you're using the extension

Optional auto-deletion policy:

We may automatically delete analytics data after 1 year of inactivity (no logins or exports). You will be notified before deletion.

8. Security

Your data is protected by:

  • ✓ HTTPS encryption (all data in transit)
  • ✓ Google's infrastructure (encrypted at rest)
  • ✓ Role-based access control (only developer access)
  • ✓ Chrome's token management (OAuth tokens)
  • ✓ No public APIs (data not exposed)

What we don't do:

  • ✗ Store passwords
  • ✗ Use unsecured connections
  • ✗ Share credentials

9. Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If we learn a child has provided data, we will delete it immediately.
